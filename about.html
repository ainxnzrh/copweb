About

RUMAH PUTER HARAPAN RANAU, SABAH

Rumah Putera Harapan (RPH) is a children's shelter in Sabah, especially in Ranau district. RPH was founded by an Islamic NGO and opened on July 12, 2008. This orphange is for children who grow up without parents (orphans), death of one of them (orphans), victims of divorce and poverty tend not to receive a perfect education.
