RUMAH PUTER HARAPAN RANAU, SABAH


OBJECTIVE

♦ Provide protection and educational opportunities to abandoned and marginalized children in education because they are shackled by poverty problems in order to continue learning perfectly.
♦ Give options to mothers, fathers or caregivers who wish to place their children in a safer and more secure place to live under good care.
♦ Provide educational assistance by conducting tuisyen and guidance classes that will be operated by qualified teachers.
